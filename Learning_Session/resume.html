Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

About Me

A. P. J. Abdul Kalam was an Indian scientist, aerospace engineer, and the 11th President of India.

He was known as the "Missile Man of India" because of his contributions to India's missile and space programs.

He inspired millions of students through his speeches, books, and dedication to education.
